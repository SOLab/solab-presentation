About SOLab

Established in 2011 as the result of "Mega-grant" competition,
SOLab is a division of the Russian State Hydrometeorological University.
Our Leading scientist is Bertrand Chapron (IFREMER, France).
Our team is from scientists, software developers and Ph.D. students.

SOLab activities:

  • Research
  • Education
  • Geo web services:
    • Data access
    • Data visualization
    • Env. monitoring
